Courses we offer

The campus has a variety of schools including SIST,SASS and SoE

SIST

SIST stands for the School of Information Science and Technology.There are various courses offered in this school including Bachelor of science in Software engineering,Computer science Applied Computer Science and Information technology

SASS

SASS stands for the School of Arts and Social Sciences.There are a variety of degree courses offered in this school such as Bachelor of arts in Music ,French ,Interior design and Criminology

SoE

SoE stands for the School of Education.There are a variety of degree courses offered in this school such as Bachelor of education in Music ,French ,Science and Arts
